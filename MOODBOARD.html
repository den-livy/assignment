COLLAPSIBLE WATER BOTTLE

Investigation / Inspiration / Discovery - Figure 1

Problem Identification
Heavy backpack with rigid bottle taking up space
Water damage from leaky bottles in bags
Difficult-to-clean traditional bottle interior
"Too heavy when carrying laptop + books" | "Leaks in my bag every week" | "Can't reach inside to clean properly - develops smell"
Existing Solutions Research
Current collapsible bottles
(Que Bottle, Nomader, Hydaway)
✓ Space-saving
✗ Still hard to clean
✗ Weak closure systems
Traditional rigid bottles
(Nalgene, Hydro Flask)
✓ Durable & leakproof
✗ Bulky when empty
✗ Heavy
Design Inspiration
Minimalist product design
(Muji, Apple)
Soft-touch materials
(Matte silicone finishes)
Modular designs
(Easy disassembly)
User Context & Daily Scenarios
Commuter on public transit with full backpack
Student at library desk - laptop + books + bottle
Gym bag with minimal essentials
TARGET USER: Urban professionals and students who prioritize portability, cleanliness, and minimalist aesthetics. Every item must justify its weight and space.
Key Design Questions
• How can we reduce BOTH weight AND volume?
• Can we create a leakproof seal that's also easy to operate?
• How do we design for disassembly to enable thorough cleaning?
• What materials balance durability, safety, and tactile appeal?
